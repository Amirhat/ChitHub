# Code of Conduct

ChitHub adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)
(version 2.1) as its Code of Conduct. In short: be respectful, be welcoming, and
assume good intent — everyone here is trying to make the project better.

## Our standards

We want taking part in ChitHub — issues, pull requests and discussions — to be a
friendly and welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background or
experience level. Please be considerate, constructive, and patient with others.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, please report it
confidentially to **a.h.amani.t@gmail.com**. Reports are handled discreetly and
reviewed as promptly as possible.

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying these standards and may remove, edit,
or reject contributions, and may temporarily or permanently restrict anyone for
behaviour they consider inappropriate.

The full text of the Contributor Covenant v2.1 — including the detailed
guidelines and enforcement steps — is available at the link above.
